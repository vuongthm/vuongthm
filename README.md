## About Me

I'm a Network Engineering student at **Vietnam–Korea University of Information and Communication Technology** (University of Danang), building a career around designing, implementing, and securing network infrastructure.

My approach is simple: understand the system deeply before touching it, document everything, and always think one step ahead about what could go wrong — and how to prevent it.

> *"A network engineer who understands security is worth two specialists."*

- 🎯 **Goal:** Network Engineer with a security-aware mindset
- 🌱 **Currently:** Advancing in routing & switching, firewall configuration, and traffic analysis
- 📍 **Based in:** Da Nang, Vietnam
